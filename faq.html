Frequently Asked Questions

How to order?

Fill the Order Form or send a message on WhatsApp or email us with your name, class, address, the items you want to buy, and their quantity.

What are the payment methods?

Currently, we accept only cash on delivery. You may also give pre orders.

What is the delivery time?

Orders delivered to school are fulfilled within the next school day. Home deliveries are given within 24 hours.

What if my order is incomplete?

If your order is incomplete, please contact us immediately through WhatsApp or email, and we'll resolve the issue InshaAllah.
